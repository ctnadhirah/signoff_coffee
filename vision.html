Our Vision, Mission & Objectives

Vision

  • To be the most approachable mobile coffee brand that delivers joy in every cup through memorable "kopi tika off" moments.

Mission

  • To serve fresh, affordable coffee with fast and friendly service through mobile setups like carboot stalls and pop-up booths.
  • To provide a cozy and welcoming coffee experience inspired by moments of rest and relaxation.
  • To combine barista skills and culinary knowledge to deliver satisfying drinks on the go.

Objectives

  • To introduce “Kopi Tika Off” as a signature concept that highlights taking a break with coffee.
  • To join more local events such as university festivals, wedding catering, and community markets.
  • To maintain quality and consistency while expanding the drink menu.
