About Signoff Coffee

Signoff Coffee was founded by Mr. Mohamad Hilmi Bin Mohamad, a seafarer with a strong passion for coffee and food. He worked as a second chef on board ships, where he spent months at sea preparing meals in fast-paced kitchen environments. During his off-duty periods, usually 3 months after 6 months of sailing, he decided to make the most of his time at home by starting a small business. That was when Signoff Coffee was born.

The name “Signoff” comes from a term commonly used among those who work at sea, where “sign on” means reporting for duty and “sign off” means returning home. For Mr. Hilmi, every sign off is not just a break, but a time to connect with people through coffee. That is how the brand’s tagline “kopi tika off” came to life, reminding people to enjoy a good cup of coffee during their break, off day, or anytime they need to pause.

Mr. Hilmi holds a Diploma in Culinary Arts and has also worked in cafes as both a barista and kitchen crew, gaining hands-on experience in both food and coffee preparation. Signoff Coffee officially opened on 7 September 2024, starting as a humble carboot stall. Since then, it has joined events like FESKO UiTM Machang, handled wedding catering, and participated in various pop-up booths. He continues to serve each cup with care, blending his sea stories, culinary skills, and passion into every sip.
